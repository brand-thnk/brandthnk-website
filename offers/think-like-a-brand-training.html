Team Training

Think Like a Brand, Not a Bank Workshop

The Two Phases

Foundation (Sessions 1-5)

Kicking off in person and continuing virtually. Each session applies the eight frameworks from the book to your institution's actual brand and positioning challenges.

Application (Sessions 6-10)

The team takes the frameworks into live decisions: messaging reviews, positioning questions, and the internal alignment conversations that keep slipping.

Teams of 4 to 12 leave with a 90-day plan, owners assigned, and the shared language to keep moving.

The Pattern

The most effective B2B fintechs don't copy-paste their website message to LinkedIn. They orchestrate distinct narratives tailored to each channel's psychology.

Website: formal, outcome-driven, infrastructure language. LinkedIn: peer-to-peer, insight-led, first-person plural. Ads: urgent, ROI-focused, boardroom language. Thought Leadership: educational, research-backed, category-creating.

The companies with "Very High" consistency scores (nCino, Alkami, Verafin, Backbase) aren't repeating the same message. They're threading a core concept ("Agentic Banking", "Anticipatory Banking", "Engagement Banking") through channel-appropriate dialects.

Klarivis Executive Visibility Engine

Prepared by: Allison Netzer, BrandThnk

Prepared for: Klarivis (Kim & Amber)

Proposed start: August 2026

Objective

We'll transform Klarivis's existing executive visibility into a durable pipeline asset by designing the infrastructure, systems, and operating model required to capture and commercialize market attention with direct alignment to the Klarivis Data and AI Institute as a lead-generation flywheel.

Why this matters

Kim and Amber generate visibility through speaking, travel, customer interaction, and industry participation. But visibility alone doesn't create pipeline. Without an underlying engine to capture insight, structure engagement, and convert attention into commercial momentum, too much value gets left on the table.

This engagement builds that engine: a practical system that increases impact from executive time, improves the quality of inbound opportunity, and gives Klarivis a repeatable process the team can run internally.

Scope of Work

1. Strategic Architecture & Opportunity Audit — $16,500

  • Audit current inbound flow to baseline lead volume and ICP fit
  • Conduct a commercial architecture exercise to map Kim's current visibility and define the path from awareness to monetized engagement
  • Inventory approximately 15 recent speaking engagements, appearances, or audience touchpoints for repurposing and repositioning
  • Define success metrics around conversations participated in, not posting frequency or follower growth
  • Identify a target list of relevant influencer, academic, and AI-adjacent voices for future amplification
  • Ensure recommendations align with the Klarivis Data and AI Institute so the system supports broader lead-generation goals

2. Executive Engine Build — $19,500

  • Design the raw-material capture system around each executive's working style
  • Build an executive LinkedIn engagement system for Kim and Amber
  • Create a lightweight nurture workflow for high-intent engagers using software support where appropriate
  • Create the distribution system required for marketing to convert executive insight into channel-ready assets efficiently
  • Develop a realistic operating cadence that's authentic, sustainable, and high impact
  • Recommend how outside experts, academics, or fellows could strengthen Institute credibility and market reach
  • Identify how Institute activity and executive visibility should reinforce one another

Deliverables

  • Operational executive visibility engine
  • Documented workflows for capture, review, packaging, and distribution
  • Executive engagement system ready for internal use
  • Target influencer/academic list with outreach recommendations
  • KPI framework and reporting cadence
  • Institute-aligned activation recommendations
  • Final strategic guidance for internal handoff and execution

What I'll need from Klarivis

  • ICBA Q&A document
  • Klarivis Data and AI Institute framework materials
  • Existing content and event archives
  • Access to LinkedIn profiles, or exported/copied profile information
  • Access to relevant historical speaking topics, appearances, and existing thought leadership materials
  • Reasonable executive review time for feedback and approvals

Exclusions

Unless added by separate agreement, this proposal doesn't include:

  • Daily social media management
  • Full ghostwriting of all executive posts
  • Paid media strategy or management
  • CRM implementation
  • Web development
  • Institute launch execution beyond activation recommendations

Closing

This engagement gives Klarivis more than content support. It creates the underlying system for a more scalable and commercial form of executive visibility.

The outcome is a repeatable engine Klarivis can use to convert attention into access, access into conversation, and conversation into pipeline — without requiring Kim and Amber to operate like full-time content creators.

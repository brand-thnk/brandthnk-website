The opportunity

MembersFirst has built something members trust. The challenge is the gap between what you've built and what the market understands you've built.

In a category where most institutions sound the same, sameness is expensive. It shows up as harder member acquisition, slower growth in the products that matter, and a story that depends on the people in the room to land. When the brand can't carry the message on its own, every campaign starts from zero.

The Breakthrough closes that gap. In eight weeks we'll define what makes MembersFirst different, build the messaging and go-to-market system to express it, and hand your team a playbook they can run without us.

How the Breakthrough works

This is a focused sprint, not an open-ended retainer. We work in four phases, each producing a concrete asset your team keeps and uses. We move from clarity, to positioning, to activation, to handoff, so the work is owned internally by the time we're done.

Phase One — Discovery and Diagnosis

We start by getting underneath the brand. Stakeholder interviews across leadership, marketing, and frontline, plus a messaging audit of how MembersFirst shows up today versus how the market reads it. We surface where members agree, where they don't, and where the real differentiation lives.

You get: a Strategy Brief that distills the insight, aligns the team on goals, and sets a shared lens for the work ahead.

Phase Two — Positioning and Narrative

We define the core narrative and the messaging hierarchy underneath it: what MembersFirst stands for, who it serves best, and the proof that makes the claim credible. This becomes the spine every campaign, branch, and conversation pulls from.

You get: a Brand Positioning Map and Messaging Framework your team can apply across every channel.

Phase Three — Go-to-Market and Activation

Positioning only matters if it ships. We translate the narrative into a practical activation model: which channels, which audiences, which hooks, and how to turn member trust into advocacy and referral. The plan is built around your real capacity, not an agency wish list.

You get: a 90-Day GTM Activation Plan with milestones, roles, and content hooks, plus a Distribution and Advocacy Toolkit.

Phase Four — Enablement and Handoff

We coach your team to run the system, document how it works, and set the measurement that tells you it's working. We close with an executive session that maps the next moves and confirms ownership inside MembersFirst.

You get: an Internal Playbook with training, a KPI and measurement framework, and an Executive Debrief that puts the strategy in leadership's hands.

What you walk away with

  • A 90-Day Go-to-Market Activation Plan

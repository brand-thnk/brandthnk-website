Kerry Murphy Findings

Customer Relations • March 2, 2026

Current Customer Service Reality

  • 19 years at Horicon Bank, moved from frontline training to customer relations
  • Frontline teams identify customers by demographics/life events, not personas
  • "This person is buying a house" → ABC process
  • "55 or better" → certain products
  • Not using persona terminology at all

Customer Pain Points & Satisfaction

  • Unhappy customers: Confused about new money transfer methods, online banking complexity, policy misunderstandings
  • Happy customers: Love staff personality and community presence, seeing bank employees at local events
  • Products "good enough" - competitive but not flashy
  • Transaction volume down, but service complexity up

Geographic & Demographic Challenges

  • Dodge County: Rural, simple lifestyle preferences, multi-generational values
  • Milwaukee expansion: Different senior demographics, more diversity, requires different approach
  • Mortgage strength not translating to market awareness
  • "Best bank for mortgages" but not top-of-mind for home buyers

2026 Senior Market Focus

  • Kerry's focus: Expand senior customers in Milwaukee County
  • Events planned: Senior center symposiums, bingo sponsorship
  • Exploring partnerships with assisted living facilities
  • Considering dedicated "Golden Free Club" Facebook page

Cassie Voight Findings

Operations Officer VP • March 20, 2026

Persona Assessment & Gaps

  • Current personas are too similar with overlapping age ranges
  • Missing commercial banking segments due to data source limitations
  • "It just doesn't define anything" - persona clarity lacks
  • Data constraints limit segment granularity
  • Need for business banking persona explicitly identified

Internal Adoption Reality

  • Personas known to senior management and some marketing staff
  • Frontline and back office would not recognize persona language
  • "Squirrel problem" with constantly changing priorities
  • Lack of consistent communication across departments
  • Strategy visibility decreases as it flows down organization

Total Expert Platform Adoption

  • Low accountability for Total Expert adoption across organization
  • Platform in place for years but real usage only in last 12-18 months
  • Danielle Schraufnagel doing excellent work but broader adoption lags
  • Retail staff using more than business bankers
  • Potential for expanded deployment across teams

Youth Banking as Top Priority

  • Top 90-day sprint recommendation: Youth Banking
  • Personal motivation: "My 16-year-old daughter knows zero about banking"
  • Young people getting financial knowledge from TikTok instead
  • Greenlight is pulling money from traditional banking
  • Created "My Money" checking with auto-age-18 transition
  • Vision: Themed app with integrated financial literacy education

Operational Simplification Imperative

  • "Keep it simple, stupid. We have to just simplify things and make it easier."
  • Too many personas, too many priorities, too many names
  • Simplicity as key to frontline adoption and execution
  • Complexity is barrier to staff engagement

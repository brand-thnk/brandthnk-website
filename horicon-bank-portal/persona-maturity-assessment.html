Persona Maturity Assessment

Pre-kickoff assessment • February 17, 2026

Overall Assessment

Horicon has good building blocks but hasn't yet done the synthesis work to turn segmentation data into actionable personas.

✅ What Horicon Has (Strengths)

Strategy & Planning

  • Clear vision: "persona-led growth engine"
  • Strategic themes defined (Customer Experience, Community Engagement, Competitive Differentiation, Tech Integration)
  • Operating model emerging (Themes → Programs → Campaigns)
  • Governance structure (monthly standups, quarterly reviews)
  • Specific campaign calendar with KPIs
  • 130th Anniversary as unifying narrative — smart positioning

Tech Stack

  • Total Expert, Alkami, Segmint, AnalyzerIQ, Vertical IQ
  • They're not flying blind on data

Segmentation Work

  • 5 consumer life stages (Wealthy Achievers, Upscale Empty Nesters, Midscale Matures, Flourishing Families, Upwardly Mobile)
  • 3 targeted retail segments (Leasing Luxury, School Daze, Fiscal Rookies)
  • 5 business sectors (Professional Services, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Agriculture, Other Services)

⚠️ Key Gaps

Area Best Practice Horicon Today
Consolidation Synthesized down to 3–4 core personas Still ~13 segments (5 life stages + 3 retail + 5 business)
Persona type Synthesized, human-centered Segmentation tool output (life stages, NAICS codes)
Journey connection Each persona maps to specific journeys Journeys mentioned but not mapped to personas
Aspiration framing Goals and narrative arc before demographics Demographically driven
Operating model Cross-functional activation Marketing-only hierarchy (Themes → Programs → Campaigns)

The Real Gap

Horicon has segments, not personas.

The current work describes who customers are demographically but doesn't answer:

  • What are they trying to accomplish?
  • What's the narrative arc of their financial life?
  • How does Horicon fit into their story, not just their wallet?

The discipline of consolidation and aspiration-first framing is the work that hasn't been done yet.

Validation Sprint Opportunity

This is exactly what the engagement is for. Horicon has strong raw materials — the goal is to help them:

  • Synthesize segments into 3–4 actionable personas
  • Add aspirational framing
  • Connect personas to journeys
  • Clarify the internal operating model beyond marketing

Good starting point. Not starting from zero.

Persona Development Strategy

Purpose Statement

The persona strategy focuses on understanding customer needs and behaviors to create personalized experiences that build trust and loyalty. Three stated goals: humanizing customer experience, data-driven persona creation, and strategic business outcomes.

Success Metrics

  • Deposit Growth — track net deposit growth monthly and annually within target opportunity segments
  • Share of Wallet Analysis — monitor average deposit share of wallet and products per household
  • Onboarding Activation Rate — measure engagement rates of new customer onboarding
  • Digital Adoption & Engagement — analyze platform logins and engagement

Implementation Timeline

  • December 2025: Initial Alignment & Governance
  • January 2026: Persona Development & Training
  • February 2026: Pilot Campaign Launch
  • Q2 2026 onward: Scale & Continuous Improvement

Key Insight

The current personas describe who customers are but not what they're trying to become. The path forward requires moving from demographic segmentation to aspiration-first personas that capture the narrative arc of customers' financial lives and position Horicon as a partner in their progress.

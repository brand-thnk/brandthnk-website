Project Timeline

1-2
Deep Discovery
March 20-31, 2026
Understand your business DNA, audience mindset, and market context.
  • Stakeholder interviews (execs, sales, product, customers)
  • Quick audit of existing brand, messaging, GTM plans
  • Identify internal blockers and external whitespace
  • Define goals and what "breakthrough" looks like
Deliverable
Strategy Brief – distilled insights, aligned goals, and a shared lens
3
Positioning Lab
April 1-4, 2026
Find the sharpest, clearest articulation of your brand's "why now"
  • Messaging sprint: core narrative, proof points, and audience resonance
  • Clarify what you are (and are not)
  • Build a messaging hierarchy: vision-level to campaign-ready
Deliverable
Brand Positioning Map + Messaging Framework (ready to plug into GTM, content, sales enablement)
4
Go-to-Market Model
April 7-11, 2026
Build a practical and punchy GTM plan rooted in your business reality
  • Select best-fit channels for traction, not noise
  • Align product moments with marketing moments
  • Define "launch-ready" and what follows it
Deliverable
90-Day GTM Activation Plan (with milestones, roles, content hooks, and external leverage points)
5
Signal Amplification
April 14-18, 2026
Get others talking about you (not just you talking about you)
  • Plan for third-party distribution (media, influencers, analysts)
  • Outline content placement and PR strategies
  • Identify internal champions and external advocates
Deliverable
Distribution & Advocacy Toolkit (third-party channels, media angles, customer-led stories)
6
Strategy-to-Execution Bridge
April 21-25, 2026
Turn the strategy into scalable action
  • Work with internal teams to prep activation
  • Set up internal documentation for handoff
  • Coach teams/agencies on adoption and adaptation
Deliverable
Internal Playbook + Training Session
7
Feedback Loop Setup
April 28-May 2, 2026
Ensure the strategy can flex with the market
  • Set up measurable KPIs tied to business outcomes
  • Build a rhythm for reflection and course correction
  • Define what "working" actually looks like (and what it doesn't)
Deliverable
Impact Dashboard Template (with guidance on how to read, report, and act on it)
8
Executive Strategy Session
May 5-7, 2026
Align leadership and energize go-forward motion
  • 60-90 minute working session with C-suite or GTM leaders
  • Present final strategy and make the case for change
  • Map next-phase priorities and ownership
Deliverable
Executive Debrief Presentation (slides + spoken walkthrough + next steps checklist)

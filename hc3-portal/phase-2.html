Scope of Work

1. Website Implementation Options

We can implement your website using either approach based on your technical preferences and team setup:

Option A: External Site + HubSpot Integration

  • Build standalone website connected to your HubSpot instance
  • Configure seamless lead capture and form integration
  • Set up tracking, attribution, and analytics
  • Full design flexibility and custom functionality

Option B: HubSpot Native Website

  • Build website directly within HubSpot's CMS platform
  • Native integration with all HubSpot marketing tools
  • Built-in forms, tracking, and lead management
  • Easier for team to maintain and update content

We'll determine the best approach during our kickoff based on your team's technical capabilities and long-term goals.

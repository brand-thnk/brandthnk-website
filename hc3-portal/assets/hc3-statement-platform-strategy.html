HC3 Statement Platform Strategy

Acquisition & Integration Playbook
Prepared by: BrandThnk | April 2026

Strategic Foundation

HC3 will build the complete statement solution through strategic acquisitions that enhance statement processing capabilities. This playbook defines how acquisitions integrate into the HC3 platform while maintaining the "Make a Statement" brand promise.

Core Positioning

Market Category: The Complete Statement Solution

Brand Promise: Make a Statement (unchanged)

Value Proposition: One platform for your complete statement process - from creation to payment to analytics

Statement-Centric Focus

HC3 will not pursue broad "operational efficiency platform" positioning. Instead, we own the statement category completely by acquiring capabilities that enhance statement processing, distribution, payment, and analytics.

Strategic Rationale:

  • Defensible market position based on existing expertise
  • Clear acquisition filter and integration roadmap
  • Unified customer story: "HC3 handles everything statement-related"
  • Competitive differentiation vs. point solution providers

Acquisition Evaluation Criteria

Statement Platform Fit Assessment

✓ Statement Workflow Enhancement: Does this capability make the complete statement process more efficient?
✓ Customer Base Alignment: Do they serve community banks and credit unions ($200M-$5B assets)?
✓ Integration Feasibility: Can this capability connect with existing HC3 statement platform?
✓ Cross-Sell Opportunity: Would existing HC3 customers benefit from this statement capability?
✓ Competitive Defensibility: Does this strengthen HC3's "complete statement solution" position?
✓ Cultural Fit: Do they share HC3's commitment to statement excellence and efficiency?

Acquisition Filter Questions

Primary Filter: "Does this make HC3's statement platform more complete?"

Secondary Filter: "Can we demonstrate statement workflow efficiency gains to customers?"

Strategic Filter: "Does this strengthen our competitive position in statement solutions?"

Revenue & Growth Strategy

Platform Economics Model

HC3's complete statement solution creates stronger economic moats than point solution competitors:

Revenue Acceleration

  • Cross-sell all statement capabilities to existing customers
  • Higher contract values through complete solution pricing
  • Faster new customer acquisition through comprehensive offering

Customer Retention

  • Higher switching costs with integrated statement workflow
  • Single vendor relationship reduces churn risk
  • Platform network effects increase with usage

Market Position Goals

  • 18-Month Target: "HC3 is the complete statement solution" becomes market consensus
  • Competitive Position: Force competitors to explain why customers need multiple vendors for statements
  • Customer Perception: HC3 is the obvious choice for any statement-related need
  • Acquisition Premium: Statement-related companies seek HC3 for platform access

Implementation Roadmap

Next 18 Months: Foundation Building

Phase 1: Platform Message (Months 1-6)

  • Update all marketing materials to "complete statement solution" positioning
  • Develop integration stories for existing HC3 capabilities
  • Create acquisition evaluation framework and deal pipeline
  • Establish statement platform metrics and success measures

Phase 2: First Acquisition (Months 6-12)

  • Complete first strategic acquisition using this playbook
  • Execute Day 1 integration and customer communication plan
  • Demonstrate cross-platform efficiency gains to customers
  • Document lessons learned for future acquisitions

Phase 3: Platform Expansion (Months 12-18)

  • Complete second acquisition to further enhance statement platform
  • Prove "complete statement solution" differentiation in market
  • Scale customer success stories across integrated platform
  • Establish HC3 as definitive statement platform leader

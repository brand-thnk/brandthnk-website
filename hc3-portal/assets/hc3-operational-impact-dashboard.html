EOS Level 10 Meeting Agenda

90-Minute Weekly Format

1. Segue (5 minutes)

Good news: Personal and professional updates. Share wins, good news, anything positive.

2. Scorecard Review (5 minutes)

Numbers only: Each owner reports their number. No discussion unless red. Note issues for later.

3. Rock Review (5 minutes)

On track/Off track only: Each rock owner gives quick status. Off-track rocks become issues.

4. Customer/Employee Headlines (5 minutes)

Critical updates: Anything the leadership team needs to know about customers or employees.

5. To-Do List (5 minutes)

Completion review: Check off completed items. Note incomplete items as issues if overdue.

6. IDS (Issues, Discuss, Solve) (60 minutes)

The heart of the meeting: Prioritize issues list, discuss top 3-5 issues, solve each one completely.

7. Conclude (5 minutes)

Recap and commit: Review to-dos, cascading messages, confirm next meeting rating (1-10).

Quarterly Rock Setting (4-Hour Session)

Review previous quarter: Complete rock review and lessons learned (30 minutes)

Establish priorities: Identify 3-7 most important priorities for next quarter (90 minutes)

SMART check: Ensure each rock is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Realistic, Timely (60 minutes)

Owner assignment: Each rock gets one clear owner who is accountable (30 minutes)

Quarterly planning: Break rocks into monthly milestones and weekly actions (90 minutes)

Risk Management & Contingencies

Stakeholder Availability Risk

Limited availability of key stakeholders could impact interview scheduling and feedback cycles.

Mitigation: Front-load stakeholder interviews in Week 1, maintain flexible scheduling options, and prepare asynchronous feedback mechanisms.
Competitive Intelligence Gaps

Incomplete competitive information could limit positioning differentiation opportunities.

Mitigation: Use multiple research sources, leverage industry networks, and focus on observable competitive behaviors and positioning.
Messaging Alignment Challenges

Diverse stakeholder perspectives could create challenges in message alignment and approval.

Mitigation: Structured workshop methodology, clear decision-making hierarchy, and incremental consensus building throughout phases.
Implementation Capacity Constraints

Limited internal resources could impact post-engagement implementation effectiveness.

Mitigation: Prioritized implementation roadmap, phased approach, and comprehensive training and documentation handoff.

Success Criteria & Measurement

Primary Success Metrics

  • Strategic Clarity - Leadership team alignment on brand positioning and competitive differentiation
  • Message Consistency - Unified messaging framework adopted across all communication channels
  • Implementation Readiness - Team equipped with tools, guidelines, and confidence to execute strategy
  • Competitive Differentiation - Clear articulation of unique value versus key market competitors

Measurement Framework

  • Stakeholder Satisfaction Surveys - End-of-phase feedback on clarity, usefulness, and actionability
  • Message Testing Results - Stakeholder validation of positioning and messaging effectiveness
  • Implementation Adoption - Tracking of strategy application across marketing materials and communications
  • Competitive Positioning Assessment - Validation of differentiation clarity versus market alternatives

Long-term Success Indicators

Post-engagement indicators of brand strategy success (tracked by Narmi team):

  • Increased message consistency across marketing materials and communications
  • Improved competitive win rates and sales cycle clarity
  • Enhanced internal team confidence in brand positioning and messaging
  • Measurable improvement in website engagement and conversion metrics

Platform-First Strategy

"Scream platform from the rooftops in a way the market and Narmi's sellers can repeat"

Platform Messaging Objective

Sharpen Narmi One platform positioning to elevate platform benefits earlier in the sales process and marketing funnel, with emphasis on efficiency and flexibility during uncertain times.

Current State Assessment

Good News: Narmi One is working - sellers understand it, it's on the website and in sales decks, people are genuinely knowledgeable about it.

Challenge: Should be "screaming platform from the rooftops" more than currently doing. With Alkami pitching One Platform post-Mantl, Narmi should be crushing them 1:1 on platform features.

Competitive Reality

Deal Dynamics: Most deals come down to Narmi, Alkami, or Q2 in banks. Platform advantages are stronger 1:1 than Alkami's - need to market the hell out of this advantage.

Key Success Criteria

  • Competitive Dominance - Crush Alkami One Platform 1:1 on platform features
  • Early Differentiation - Platform benefits communicated upfront in sales conversations
  • Marketing Team Understanding - Internal team fully grasps and articulates platform advantages
  • Sales Enablement - Team equipped to destroy Alkami's platform marketing

Step 2: Select Core Personas (3-5 Maximum)

Focus beats coverage. Better to deeply understand 3 buyers than superficially know 10. Choose personas that represent 60%+ of your ideal deals.

Selection Strategy
Start with personas that match your best existing customers. Who are your easiest sales? Who stays longest? Who refers others? Find those patterns in the Litmus library and prioritize similar profiles.

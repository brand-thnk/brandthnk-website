Paid Ads Playbook

Digital Onboarding (digitalonboarding.com)
Prepared by BrandThnk | March 2026

Use this to run LinkedIn Ads and Google Ads for Digital Onboarding in the United States.

1. Goal, Audience, and Competitive Positioning

Primary Goal

  • LinkedIn: Generate qualified leads for banks, credit unions, and fintech/core partners (demo requests, content downloads, webinar sign-ups).
  • Google: Capture in-market searchers for "digital onboarding" and "customer engagement" solutions for financial institutions.

Core Audience Segments

  • Banks: CMOs, VP/Director of Marketing, CX, Digital Banking, Product, IT.
  • Credit unions: CEOs, Presidents, COOs, VP of Operations, VP of Digital Banking, Branch Managers.
  • Partners: Fintechs and core banking providers (Partnerships, Product, Growth roles).

Competitive Positioning (from BrandThnk analysis)

  • Focus on digital adoption journeys that drive measurable outcomes (direct deposit, eStatements, product adoption), not just "onboarding".
  • Emphasize:
    • Bank-ready, compliant journeys for financial institutions.
    • Light-touch integration and no heavy IT lift.
    • Proven lifts in adoption and engagement.
2. LinkedIn Ads - Campaign Structure

Lead Gen - Digital Onboarding for Banks & Credit Unions

Objective: Lead Generation (or Website Visits if you're early).

Ad Set 1: Bank CMOs - Adoption & Activation

Audience

  • Job titles: CMO Marketing Director VP Marketing Head of Marketing
  • Companies: Banks, financial institutions
  • Location: United States

Ad 1 - Headline + Copy

Headline: Drive Digital Adoption After Account Opening

Intro line: Automate onboarding journeys for new banking customers.

Body: Help banks increase digital banking adoption, eStatement enrollment, and direct deposit sign-ups-all without extra staff. Use configurable campaigns and templates to keep messaging on-brand and compliant.

CTA: Download engagement playbook

Lead Gen Form fields: Name, Email, Company, Job Title

Ad 2 - Headline + Copy

Headline: Turn New Account Openers into Engaged Customers

Intro line: Close the gap between acquisition and activation.

Body: Stop leaving new accounts dormant. Digital Onboarding helps banks personalize journeys that drive product adoption, cross-sell, and measurable engagement. Track goals like direct deposit enrollment, CD renewals, and digital channel usage.

CTA: Request demo

Ad Set 2: Credit Union Leaders - Member Engagement

Audience

  • Job titles: CEO President COO VP of Operations VP of Digital Banking Branch Manager
  • Companies: Credit unions
  • Location: United States

Ad 1 - Headline + Copy

Headline: Reduce Friction in Credit Union Onboarding

Body: Make it easy for members to enroll in eStatements, set up direct deposit, and accept pre-approved offers-often without logging in. Digital Onboarding helps credit unions increase adoption and keep members engaged, not annoyed.

CTA: Learn more

Ad 2 - Headline + Copy

Headline: Win Back Abandoned Applicants & Drive Cross-Sell

Body: Target abandoned applicants and existing members with compliant, personalized journeys for checking, savings, credit cards, loans, and more. Leverage data-driven campaigns that increase adoption while reducing manual outreach.

CTA: Request case study

Ad Set 3: Fintech & Core Partners - Embedded Adoption

Audience

  • Job titles: Partnerships Business Development Product Manager Head of Growth Head of Product
  • Companies: Fintechs, core banking providers, banking-tech vendors
  • Location: United States

Ad 1 - Headline + Copy

Headline: Embed Digital Onboarding Into Your Stack

Body: Help banks and credit unions boost adoption and engagement without heavy customization. Use Digital Onboarding's APIs and integrations to add journeys for digital banking, eStatements, and direct deposit into your platform.

CTA: Explore integration options

5. Weekly Ad-Ops Routine
Monday
Review LinkedIn + Google Ads performance. Pause low-CTR / high-CPC ads or keywords; adjust budgets.
Wednesday
Test 1-2 new ad variations (headline, image, or CTA).
Friday
Export lead/demo data and share with sales. Update a simple dashboard (Google Sheets or Notion) with: Leads, cost per lead, demo requests, country of origin (U.S.), and segment (bank vs. CU vs. partner).

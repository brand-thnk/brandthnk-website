Nate Shahan brings proven fintech leadership and data product expertise to Digital Onboarding as Chief Product Officer. As co-founder of Segmint, he built patented engagement analytics solutions that were acquired by Alkami in a $135.5M transaction.

His experience spans data-driven customer engagement, predictive lifecycle analytics, and the practical challenges of implementing AI solutions in regulated financial environments. At Digital Onboarding, he leads product strategy focused on what happens after account opening—turning new customers into engaged, profitable relationships.

Shahan's unique perspective combines deep technical expertise with practical understanding of community bank constraints, positioning him as an authentic voice on digital banking engagement strategies that actually work.

Three Thought Leadership Topics

"It's Not About Account Opening. It's About What Happens Next."
Positioning post-onboarding engagement as the real competitive differentiator in digital banking.
  • Most banks optimize for speed of account opening, not engagement quality
  • Real ROI comes from lifecycle value, not acquisition metrics
  • Data shows: engaged customers are 3x more profitable
  • Framework: Onboard → Engage → Optimize → Grow
"Small Teams, Measurable Growth: The Engagement Math"
How community banks can achieve enterprise-level engagement outcomes with limited resources.
  • Automation that feels personal, not robotic
  • Focus on high-impact touchpoints vs. constant communication
  • Metrics that matter: engagement depth, not frequency
  • Case studies: 40% increase in cross-sell with 60% less effort
"Before You Deploy AI, Connect Your Data"
Practical approach to AI implementation that starts with data infrastructure, not technology.
  • AI without unified data is just expensive automation
  • Three-layer approach: Data → Intelligence → Action
  • Common pitfalls: Technology-first vs. outcome-first implementation
  • Real-world examples from community bank implementations

Report Format & Samples

Executive-ready research reports with direct quotes, strategic analysis, and numbered recommendations tied to specific findings.

What's Included in Every Report

Each BrandThnk Insights report is structured for executive consumption, featuring evidence-based findings and actionable recommendations.

1. Executive Summary

3-4 sentences presenting key findings upfront for stakeholder communication and decision-making.

2. Key Findings

3-5 findings with direct persona quotes and clear verdicts: strong resonance, mixed reaction, does not resonate, or gap identified.

3. Cross-Persona Comparison

Matrix showing how each persona reacted to each test element, revealing segment-specific patterns and opportunities.

4. Strategic Analysis

Interpretation of findings beyond surface responses, connecting insights to business implications and competitive context.

5. Recommendations

3-5 specific, actionable numbered recommendations tied directly to research findings with supporting evidence.

6. Methodology Disclosure

Transparency about what synthetic research can and cannot do, including limitations and confidence levels.

Sample Persona Responses

Real examples of how personas respond during research interviews, demonstrating the specificity and authenticity of insights generated.

Transparency & Limitations

Every report includes clear disclosure about synthetic research methodology, confidence levels, and recommended follow-up research for critical decisions. We believe transparency about AI-simulated research capabilities is essential for ethical application.

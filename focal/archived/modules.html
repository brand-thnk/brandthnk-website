Six Research Modules

Choose the decision type you're addressing. Each module runs structured interviews with relevant personas and delivers specific insights for your strategic challenge.

MODULE 1

Message Testing

Evaluate taglines, positioning statements, and campaign messages to identify what resonates with audiences and differentiates from competitors.

Questions This Module Answers

  • Which messages resonate with your target segments?
  • What differentiates your brand from competitors?
  • Which messaging falls flat or creates confusion?
  • How do different personas interpret your positioning?

Insights Provided

Resonance levels across personas, differentiation opportunities, competitive messaging gaps, and segment-specific language preferences that drive connection.
Best For
New taglines, positioning statements, value propositions, or campaign themes before creative development begins.
MODULE 2

Product Validation

Test product concepts and feature bundles to determine customer need recognition, adoption likelihood, and pricing acceptance.

Questions This Module Answers

  • Do customers recognize the underlying need this product solves?
  • Would they adopt the solution you're proposing?
  • Is there willingness to pay at your price point?
  • Which features drive adoption vs. create barriers?

Insights Provided

Market demand validation, feature prioritization guidance, adoption barriers by segment, and pricing sensitivity across different personas.
Best For
New product concepts, feature bundling decisions, and pricing strategy before significant development investment.
MODULE 3

Competitive Positioning

Assess how your brand performs against named competitors, revealing perception gaps and switching motivators by segment.

Questions This Module Answers

  • How do customers perceive you relative to rivals?
  • Where are your differentiation gaps?
  • What triggers switching behavior in your market?
  • Which competitive strengths are most vulnerable?

Insights Provided

Perception gaps vs. competitors, competitive advantages by segment, switching drivers and barriers, and opportunities for repositioning.
Best For
Competitive response strategies, repositioning efforts, and understanding your differentiation opportunities in crowded markets.
MODULE 4

Persona Pressure Test

Validate or challenge assumptions about existing personas to uncover blind spots and missed insights.

Questions This Module Answers

  • Which persona characteristics actually hold up under scrutiny?
  • Where are the blind spots in your current understanding?
  • What assumptions need revision or elimination?
  • Are you missing key behavioral patterns?

Insights Provided

Validation of existing persona accuracy, identification of missing persona characteristics, and recommendations for persona refinement or expansion.
Best For
Annual persona reviews, strategy updates, or when campaign performance doesn't match persona predictions.
MODULE 5

Campaign Pre-Test

Evaluate a full campaign concept including creative direction, channel strategy, offer appeal, and shareability before budget commitment.

Questions This Module Answers

  • Does the creative direction work for your audience?
  • Is the channel strategy sound for reaching personas?
  • Does the offer appeal resonate and drive action?
  • Is the content shareable and memorable?

Insights Provided

Creative effectiveness ratings, channel viability assessment, offer appeal scores, and shareability potential across different segments.
Best For
Major campaign launches, significant media investments, or when testing integrated campaign concepts end-to-end.
MODULE 6

Journey Gap Analysis

Guide personas through customer journeys step-by-step to identify friction points and decision moments.

Questions This Module Answers

  • Where does friction occur in the customer journey?
  • What causes drop-off at each stage?
  • Which moments win or lose the customer?
  • How do different personas experience the same journey?

Insights Provided

Friction point identification, drop-off risk assessment, critical decision moment analysis, and persona-specific journey optimization opportunities.
Best For
Digital experience optimization, conversion improvement initiatives, and understanding experience gaps by customer segment.

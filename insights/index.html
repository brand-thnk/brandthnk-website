What BrandThnk Insights Does

BrandThnk Insights runs AI-simulated research interviews against banking customer personas that behave like real people. You input your messages, product concepts, or campaign ideas. Each persona participates in an 11-turn, three-phase interview staying in character based on documented values and behaviors.

Built for financial institutions and fintechs selling to banks. Whether you're a community bank testing a new campaign or a fintech validating your value proposition to FI buyers, our personas cover consumer banking, small business banking, and B2B fintech decision-makers.

Our personas are built from published banking data (FDIC, Federal Reserve, J.D. Power) and validated through 30+ strategy workshops with financial brands across three segments: B2B Fintech Buyers, Consumer Banking, Small Business Banking.

The system analyzes patterns across all personas to identify agreements, divergences, and strategic gaps. Receive an executive-ready document with direct quotes, comparison matrices, strategic analysis, and actionable recommendations.

We built a structured interview system, not a chatbot with banking knowledge.

The Problem You Already Know

Everyone internal loves it. Your CEO, your agency, your whole team. But you have no idea whether your customers will care.

Traditional research takes 6-12 weeks and costs $50,000 or more. Focus groups tell you what people say in a room with free sandwiches, not what they actually think. Online surveys produce data that confirms whatever you already believed.

So you skip the research. You launch. You spend the budget. And you find out whether it worked 90 days later when the numbers come in.

What BrandThnk Insights Does

BrandThnk Insights runs AI-simulated research interviews against banking customer personas that behave like real people. You input your messages, product concepts, or campaign ideas. The system interviews each persona using a structured research protocol. You get a branded report with findings, cross-segment comparison, and specific recommendations.

Research that takes 10 minutes, not 10 weeks.

We built a structured interview system, not a chatbot with banking knowledge.

How It Works

1

Choose a research module

Select the decision type you're addressing from the six available modules and customer segment types.

2

Set up your research

Enter company information, test materials, market type, competitors, and select which personas to interview.

3

System interviews each persona

Each persona participates in an 11-turn, three-phase interview staying in character based on documented values and behaviors.

4

Cross-persona synthesis

The system analyzes patterns across all personas to identify agreements, divergences, and strategic gaps.

5

Get your branded report

Receive an executive-ready document with direct quotes, comparison matrices, strategic analysis, and actionable recommendations.

Six Research Modules

Message Testing

Evaluate taglines, positioning statements, and campaign messages to identify what resonates with audiences and differentiates from competitors.

Product Validation

Test product concepts and feature bundles to determine customer need recognition, adoption likelihood, and pricing acceptance.

Competitive Positioning

Assess how your brand performs against named competitors, revealing perception gaps and switching motivators by segment.

Persona Pressure Test

Validate or challenge assumptions about existing personas to uncover blind spots and missed insights.

Campaign Pre-Test

Evaluate a full campaign concept including creative direction, channel strategy, offer appeal, and shareability before budget commitment.

Journey Gap Analysis

Guide personas through customer journeys step-by-step to identify friction points and decision moments.

What BrandThnk Insights Does

BrandThnk Insights runs AI-simulated research interviews against banking customer personas that behave like real people. You input your messages, product concepts, or campaign ideas. The system interviews each persona using a structured research protocol. You get a branded report with findings, cross-segment comparison, and specific recommendations.

Built for financial institutions and fintechs selling to banks. Whether you're a community bank testing a new campaign or a fintech validating your value proposition to FI buyers, our personas cover consumer banking, small business banking, and B2B fintech decision-makers.

Six Research Modules

Message Testing

Evaluate taglines, positioning statements, and campaign messages to identify what resonates with audiences and differentiates from competitors.

Product Validation

Test product concepts and feature bundles to determine customer need recognition, adoption likelihood, and pricing acceptance.

Competitive Positioning

Assess how your brand performs against named competitors, revealing perception gaps and switching motivators by segment.

Persona Pressure Test

Validate or challenge assumptions about existing personas to uncover blind spots and missed insights.

Campaign Pre-Test

Evaluate a full campaign concept including creative direction, channel strategy, offer appeal, and shareability before budget commitment.

Journey Gap Analysis

Guide personas through customer journeys step-by-step to identify friction points and decision moments.

Research Report: Message Testing
Generated March 18, 2026

Executive Summary

Testing revealed strong resonance with relationship-focused messaging among Steady Stewards and Community Anchors, but significant resistance to rate-focused positioning across all digital-native personas.

Key Findings

Strong Resonance: "Community-focused banking"

"I want a bank that understands my business and my community, not one that treats me like an account number." — Community Anchor

Mixed Reaction: "Digital-first experience"

"Digital is fine, but when my loan gets complicated, I need a human who knows my business." — Main Street Operator

Does Not Resonate: "Best rates in town"

"Everyone says that. Show me the numbers and let me do the math myself." — Digital Pragmatist

Recommendations

  1. Lead with relationship messaging for traditional banking channels and community-focused campaigns.
  2. Provide specific rate data rather than generic "best rates" claims when targeting digital-savvy segments.
  3. Position digital as enhancement, not replacement, for business banking communications.

Research Methodology

A structured, data-driven approach to AI-simulated persona interviews that delivers reliable insights for strategic decisions.

This is a research methodology, not a prompt generator
Our approach uses structured interview protocols, validated personas, and systematic analysis to ensure research quality and reliability. Each interview follows established research principles adapted for AI-simulated interactions.

Three-Phase Interview Protocol

Each persona participates in an 11-turn interview that adapts based on responses, featuring conditional follow-up logic that explores competitive mentions, surfaced objections, and unexpected insights.

Phase 1: Context Setting

Establishes the persona's current banking situation, emotional baseline, and relevant context for the research topic. Creates natural conversation flow and authentic engagement.

2-3 interview turns

Phase 2: Core Research

Presents research stimuli and captures reactions, comprehension, differentiation, and competitive comparison. Adapts follow-up questions based on persona responses and emerging themes.

4-8 interview turns

Phase 3: Synthesis

Uses open-ended questions to surface insights that the structured protocol might miss. Allows personas to share final thoughts and unexpected perspectives.

1-2 interview turns

57-Question Research Library

Our methodology includes 57 research questions organized across six modules, each with conditional follow-up logic:

  • Competitive Follow-ups: When personas mention competitors, subsequent questions automatically probe competitive comparison and switching factors.
  • Objection Exploration: If skepticism emerges, follow-ups explore specific concerns and barriers to adoption.
  • Clarification Probes: When responses are unclear, the system asks for specific examples and explanations.
  • Depth Questions: For strong reactions (positive or negative), additional questions uncover underlying motivations.

Systematic Analysis Process

Rather than simply summarizing responses, our analysis identifies patterns across personas and provides strategic interpretation of findings.

1

Pattern Identification

Analyze responses across all interviewed personas to identify agreements, divergences, and segment-specific patterns.

2

Resonance Classification

Classify findings by resonance level: strong positive, mixed reactions, or does not resonate, with supporting evidence.

3

Strategic Gap Analysis

Surface strategic gaps, competitive vulnerabilities, and opportunities that emerge from cross-persona comparison.

4

Insight Synthesis

Connect findings to strategic implications with numbered recommendations tied to specific evidence from interviews.

What You Receive

Each research report includes:

  • Direct Quotes: Actual persona responses that illustrate key findings
  • Cross-Persona Comparison Matrices: Side-by-side analysis of how different segments respond
  • Strategic Interpretation: What the findings mean for your business decisions
  • Numbered Recommendations: Specific actions connected to research evidence
  • Executive Summary: Key insights formatted for stakeholder communication

LoanPay Messaging Framework

Updated March 2026 with Fresh Market Data

The three core message pillars remain the foundation. Each pillar is now anchored to current market data that validates urgency and positions LoanPay as a practical solution to board-level concerns.

Primary Positioning Statement

For credit unions with member delinquencies and payment friction
Who need to reduce operational burden without adding headcount
LoanPay is a white-label loan payment platform
That makes it simple for members to pay on time across every loan type
Unlike generic payment processors and legacy core servicing
LoanPay is purpose-built for credit union outcomes: fewer delinquencies, lighter staff burden, stronger member relationships.

Three Core Message Pillars

Pillar 1: Tackle Delinquencies Where Risk Is Highest

Core Message

Help members pay on time. Protect your portfolio.

Supporting Points

  • Credit union delinquencies at highest level since 2013 (1.03%, NCUA Q4 2025)
  • Used-auto and indirect loans now flagged as "epicenter of credit risk"
  • 60+ day auto delinquencies projected to reach 1.51-1.54% by year-end 2026 (TransUnion)
  • Automated reminders and payment options reduce missed payments
  • Every day a payment is late costs the CU money and strains member relationship

Proof Required

Customer delinquency before-and-after data. Payment frequency by loan type. Member engagement metrics post-launch.

Data Reference

NCUA Q4 2025. CUNA-aligned analytics (March 2026). TransUnion forecast.

Talk Track

"Your delinquency metrics are rising just like every credit union we talk to. Used-auto is the epicenter right now. LoanPay removes the friction that causes members to fall behind on their most problematic loan segment."

Pillar 2: Turn Complex Payment Options into a Strength

Core Message

Free up your team to serve members, not process payments.

Supporting Points

  • NCUA reducing regulatory capacity (23% headcount reduction projected for 2026)
  • Staffing flat or shrinking across lending, compliance, tech roles
  • Loan payment processing remains one of the most labor-intensive manual processes
  • White-label platform eliminates branding complexity
  • Self-service reduces inbound payment calls and reconciliation overhead
  • Digital payment adoption rising sharply (60%+ of active members using digital banking)

Proof Required

Time savings estimates per CU by asset size. Staff efficiency data. Call-volume reduction metrics from current customers.

Data Reference

NCUA 2026 budget projections. Callahan staffing data. Credit union digital adoption benchmarks (March 2026).

Talk Track

"Your team can't add headcount. Your delinquencies are rising. Your members expect digital. LoanPay handles the mechanics so your staff can focus on member relationships, not payment processing."

Pillar 3: Lead with the Integration Story They Are Already Worried About

Core Message

Modernize without a core system project.

Supporting Points

  • 80 mergers consummated in 2025; consolidation increasingly framed as digital-transformation plays
  • Acquired institutions often retain legacy payment infrastructure for 12-24 months post-close
  • Post-close integration budgets allocated for "modernization and efficiency"
  • CU M&A activity accelerating (4,287 CUs, down from 4,455 a year earlier)
  • Replacement cycles align with integration planning windows
  • Cloud-based solutions now encouraged by NCUA (rather than legacy on-prem infrastructure)

Proof Required

Case studies from post-acquisition integrations. Post-close timeline and budget data from current customers.

Data Reference

NCUA count data (Q4 2025). CU M&A analytics (S&P, Callahan). NCUA Financial Technology Division guidance.

Talk Track

"If you're in a consolidation, the acquiring institution isn't going to fund your core system replacement. But they will fund integration efficiency. LoanPay solves the payment piece without the core system timeline and cost."

Audience-Specific Messaging

For CEO/Executive

Priority: Portfolio protection, strategic positioning, board credibility.

Lead with: Delinquency trends and competitive landscape. Modernization narrative in M&A context.

Avoid: Technical details. Implementation timelines.

Talk Track Starter

"Delinquencies are at levels we haven't seen since 2013. Your board is asking what you're doing about it. LoanPay is the fastest way to improve your metrics without a core system project."

For CFO/Finance

Priority: Delinquency reduction, cost savings, ROI.

Lead with: Hard numbers on delinquency impact and operational efficiency gains.

Avoid: Soft benefits without proof.

Talk Track Starter

"Used-auto delinquencies are rising. Each additional basis point of delinquency costs your credit union [X basis points of profitability]. LoanPay reduces delinquencies and staff overhead. Here's the math."

For COO/Operations

Priority: Staff efficiency, integration simplicity, support.

Lead with: Operational burden reduction and white-label simplicity.

Avoid: Strategic positioning (save for CEO).

Talk Track Starter

"Your lending team is stretched. Loan payment processing is a manual drag. LoanPay offloads the mechanics. Your team stays focused on member relationships."

For IT/Technology

Priority: Security, integration, compliance, maintenance.

Lead with: Technical specs, cloud architecture, API capabilities.

Avoid: Business outcomes (they want technical proof).

Talk Track Starter

"LoanPay runs on [cloud infrastructure]. Integration is RESTful API. We handle compliance, updates, and security. Your team can focus on strategic initiatives."

Objection Handling Framework

Objection Response Proof Needed
"We already have a payment solution" Your current solution is transaction-focused. LoanPay is outcome-focused. What's your current delinquency trend? How much staff time do you spend processing payments? Delinquency data. Staff time audit.
"It's just another payment option" Fair question. But most payment processors don't address delinquency or staffing burden. LoanPay does both. Your members expect digital. Your staff expects less overhead. Competitive contrast. Customer case studies.
"What's the ROI?" [Show specific metrics from customers]. Delinquency reduction. Staff time savings from reduced call volume. Improved member retention. Customer data. Cost savings model.
"Our members are fine with current process" Are they? What's your 30-day delinquency rate? Your member satisfaction on loan payment experience? Your digital adoption rate for payments? Benchmark data. Member survey results.
"Too complex to integrate" Integration is API-based, not a core system project. You're live in 8-12 weeks. No core system involvement required. Integration timeline. Go-live case studies.
"We're in consolidation talks" Exactly. Post-close, your acquirer will fund integration efficiency. LoanPay fits that narrative. Digital solutions are valued in M&A. M&A integration case studies.

Words We Use / Words We Avoid

Use

  • Outcomes
  • Members (not customers or users)
  • Practical
  • Purpose-built
  • White-label
  • Reduce friction
  • Delinquency
  • Operational burden
  • Modernize

Avoid

  • Revolutionary
  • Disruptive
  • Best-in-class (unless proven)
  • Seamless (overused)
  • Easy (unless specific)
  • Fintech jargon
  • Game-changer
  • Next-generation (undefined)

CheckAlt LoanPay: Brand Strategy & Market Activation

Executive Briefing • March 2026

Engagement Overview

Engagement Period

November 2025 – March 2026

Investment

Signed

BrandThnk completed a positioning and go-to-market activation engagement for LoanPay, CheckAlt's white-label loan payment platform for credit unions. The engagement focused on three core workstreams: developing differentiated messaging and positioning, designing a staged market activation strategy (Rolling Thunder), and preparing leadership alignment through an executive workshop.

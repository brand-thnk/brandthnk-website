Scope of Work

Week 1–2: Deep Discovery

Objective: Understand your business DNA, audience mindset, and market context.

  • Stakeholder interviews with execs, sales, product, customers if possible
  • Quick audit of existing brand, messaging, GTM plans
  • Identify blockers — internal blockers and external whitespace
  • Define goals and what "breakthrough" looks like

Deliverable: Strategy Brief – distilled insights, aligned goals, and a shared lens

Week 3: Positioning Lab

Objective: Find the sharpest, clearest articulation of your brand's "why now"

  • Messaging sprint: core narrative, proof points, and audience resonance
  • Clarify what you are (and are not)
  • Build a messaging hierarchy: vision-level to campaign-ready

Deliverable: Brand Positioning Map + Messaging Framework (ready to plug into GTM, content, sales enablement)

Week 4: Go-to-Market Model

Objective: Build a practical and punchy GTM plan rooted in your business reality

  • Select best-fit channels for traction, not noise
  • Align product moments with marketing moments
  • Define "launch-ready" and what follows it

Deliverable: 90-Day GTM Activation Plan (with milestones, roles, content hooks, and external leverage points)

Week 5: Signal Amplification

Objective: Get others talking about you (not just you talking about you)

  • Plan for third-party distribution (media, influencers, analysts)
  • Outline content placement and PR strategies
  • Identify internal champions and external advocates

Deliverable: Distribution & Advocacy Toolkit (third-party channels, media angles, customer-led stories)

Week 6: The Strategy-to-Execution Bridge

Objective: Turn the strategy into scalable action

  • Work with internal teams to prep activation
  • Set up internal documentation for handoff
  • Coach teams/agencies on adoption and adaptation

Deliverable: Internal Playbook + Training Session

Week 7: Feedback Loop Setup

Objective: Ensure the strategy can flex with the market

  • Set up measurable KPIs tied to business outcomes
  • Build a rhythm for reflection and course correction
  • Define what "working" actually looks like (and what it doesn't)

Deliverable: Impact Dashboard Template (with guidance on how to read, report, and act on it)

Week 8: Executive Strategy Session

Objective: Align leadership and energize go-forward motion

  • 60-90 minute working session with C-suite or GTM leaders
  • Present final strategy and make the case for change
  • Map next-phase priorities and ownership

Deliverable: Executive Debrief Presentation (slides + spoken walkthrough + next steps checklist)

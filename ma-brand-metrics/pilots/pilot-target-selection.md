**Service:** Fintech Brand Due Diligence
**Purpose:** Pilot methodology validation using realistic M&A targets
**Selection Date:** April 13, 2026
**Implementation Phase:** Week 3-4 (Pilot Target Selection & Research)

## Selection Criteria

### Target Profile Requirements
- **Size:** $50M-$500M valuation range (realistic PE/strategic acquisition targets)
- **Information Availability:** Sufficient public information for comprehensive brand analysis
- **Brand Presence:** Active social media, thought leadership, developer community engagement
- **M&A Appeal:** Strategic value for consolidation, infrastructure positioning
- **Geographic Focus:** US-based or significant US market presence
- **Category Diversity:** Different fintech sectors to test methodology versatility

### Brand Analysis Requirements
- Social media and content presence for share of voice analysis
- Customer testimonials and case studies for brand equity evaluation
- Developer/technical community presence for fintech-specific metrics
- Competitive landscape with clear positioning differentiation
- Regulatory and compliance standing for fintech risk assessment

## Methodology Testing Framework

### Phase 1: Competitive Positioning Analysis (Week 1)
**Moov Focus:** Developer-first payments infrastructure positioning vs. enterprise-sales competitors
**Sardine Focus:** AI-native compliance vs. legacy rule-based systems

**Research Threads:**
- Market category definition and size
- Competitive messaging analysis and overlap
- Capability differentiation vs. competitive claims
- Market share trends and positioning shifts

### Phase 2: Brand Strength Analysis (Week 2)
**Share of Voice Measurement:**
- Social media mentions and engagement (Brand24/Talkwalker)
- Developer community conversations (GitHub, Hacker News, Stack Overflow)
- Industry publication coverage and analyst mentions
- Conference/event presence and speaking opportunities

**Brand Equity Assessment:**
- Customer testimonials and case study analysis
- Pricing power evidence vs. commoditized alternatives
- Employee advocacy and Glassdoor sentiment
- Strategic partnership quality and integration relationships

**Fintech-Specific Metrics:**
- Trust narratives around security and compliance
- Developer community advocacy and technical credibility
- Regulatory compliance reputation and industry standing
- Crisis resilience and reputation management track record

### Phase 3: M&A Valuation Impact (Week 3)
**Enterprise Value Attribution:**
- Brand premium/discount calculation using Brand Advantage Framework
- Risk-adjusted scenario modeling (best/base/worst case)
- Integration complexity assessment
- Stakeholder perception impact on acquisition success

## Success Metrics for Pilot Phase

### Methodology Validation Criteria
- **Data Availability:** Successfully gather 80%+ of required brand metrics for both companies
- **Differentiation:** Clear identification of brand strengths/weaknesses unique to each category
- **Competitive Context:** Comprehensive competitive positioning analysis for both sectors
- **M&A Translation:** Quantifiable enterprise value impact assessment for investment decisions

### Quality Standards
- **Source Verification:** Minimum 3 independent sources for major brand strength claims
- **Quantitative Backing:** Data-driven evidence for all brand awareness and equity assertions
- **Recent Data:** 2024-2026 preferred, 2022+ acceptable for trend analysis
- **Stakeholder Perspectives:** All 5 C-suite personas interviewed for each target

### Output Requirements
- **Executive Summary:** Brand strength score (1-100) and EV impact for each company
- **Detailed Assessment:** 15-20 page report following standardized template
- **Investment Presentation:** 12-15 slide deck for investment committee review
- **Lessons Learned:** Methodology refinements and process improvements documentation
